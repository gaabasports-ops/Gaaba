Who We Are

Pakistan's Premier Combat Sports Manufacturer

Gaaba Sport is a leading manufacturer of premium boxing gloves, MMA gear, and combat sports equipment based in Sialkot, Pakistan — the world's renowned hub for sports manufacturing.

With over 15 years of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering world-class quality at competitive wholesale prices. Our products are trusted by gyms, retailers, distributors, and combat sports brands across the USA, UK, Canada, Europe, and Australia.

We specialize in both ready-stock products and complete OEM/private label manufacturing, giving our partners the flexibility to build their own brands with our craftsmanship.

Our Journey

The Gaaba Sport Timeline

2009

Humble Beginnings

Gaaba Sport started as a small leather workshop in Sialkot, Pakistan, crafting handmade boxing gloves for local gyms.

2012

First Export Order

Secured our first international order — 500 pairs of boxing gloves shipped to a gym in the United Kingdom.

2015

Expanded Product Line

Added MMA gloves, head guards, focus pads, and training equipment to our catalog. Invested in modern machinery.

2018

OEM & Private Label Launch

Launched full OEM services allowing clients to create their own branded combat sports equipment with custom designs.

2021

Global Distribution Network

Established distribution partnerships in USA, Canada, UK, Germany, France, and Australia. Reached 1000+ clients milestone.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our premium digital presence with enhanced e-commerce capabilities, real-time customer support, and streamlined ordering.

How We Work

Our Manufacturing Process

1. Material Selection

We source premium genuine leather, synthetic leather, high-density foam, and durable stitching materials from certified suppliers.

2. Pattern & Design

Expert pattern makers create precise templates. Custom designs are developed with 3D modeling and client approval.

3. Cutting & Stitching

Precision cutting followed by expert hand-stitching and machine reinforcement for maximum durability and aesthetics.

4. Quality Control

Rigorous testing including stress tests, fit checks, and visual inspections before packaging and shipping.
